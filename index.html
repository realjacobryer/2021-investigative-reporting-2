At the onset of "Turning the Tide", there were over 300 combined cluster sites and hotels being used in the NYC shelter system.

By September 2021, only six cluster sites remained open.

Hotels in use decreased, but at a slower rate.

The remaining six cluster sites were all located in the BEDCO - Bronx sites. Operations were taken over by Bronx Family Housing in 2018.

In the past five years, over 80% of all formerly operational cluster sites and hotels were no longer in use.
